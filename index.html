    Our Story

    Akili Foundation is a leading provider of addiction treatment and recovery services, as well as educational courses to help people build better lives for themselves. We are committed to helping people escape from the cycle of addiction and achieve their full potential. At Akili Foundation, we believe that recovery is not just about being sober. It's about finding your way back to yourself and living a life you love. That's why we offer a variety of programs and services to meet the individual needs of each client.

    Our Mission

    Akili Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping people overcome addiction and build better lives for themselves through comprehensive treatment programs, educational courses, and community support. We are committed to providing high-quality, affordable care that is accessible to everyone.We can change life for those who have no hope.

    Our Values

    Excellence: Akili Foundation uses evidence-based practices in all of its programs and services. The staff at Akili Foundation are highly trained and experienced, and they are committed to providing the highest quality of care. Innovation: Akili Foundation is constantly looking for new and innovative ways to help its clients recover and build better lives for themselves.
